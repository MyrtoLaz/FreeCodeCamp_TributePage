Elon Musk

A man of vision.
Elon Musk during a TED talk about his projects.

Short timeline

  • Born on June 28 1971, in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa
  • Moved to Canada in 1989
  • At age 17 he was accepted into Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario
  • In 1992 he transferred to the University of Pennsylvania
  • In 1997 he received a Bachelor of Science degree in physics from its College of Arts and Sciences and a Bachelor of Science degree in economics from its Wharton School of Business
  • In 1995 he moved to California to begin a PhD in applied physics and materials science at Stanford University which he left to pursue his entrepreneurial aspirations
  • In 2002 he became a US citizen

His career

  • In 1995 he and his brother Kimbal started Zip2
  • In 1999 he co-founded X.com
  • He founded SpaceX in May 2002
  • Tesla, Inc. was incorporated in July 2003. Musk joined Tesla's board of directors as chairman in February 2004
  • In 2005 he announced the creation of OpenAI
  • In 2006 he co-founded Neuralink and SolarCity, which was acquired by Tesla, Inc. the same year
  • In 2006 he served as a member of the United States National Academy of Sciences Aeronautics and Space Board
  • In 2008 he assumed leadership of Tesla, Inc. as CEO and product architect
  • On August 2013 Musk unveiled a concept for a high-speed transportation system - Hyperloop
  • On December 2016 The Boring Company was founded

Some awards

  • 2006 Global Green product design award and 2007 Index Design award for his design of the Tesla Roadster
  • American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics George Low award for the most outstanding contribution in the field of space transportation in 2007/2008
  • National Wildlife Federation 2008 National Conservation Achievement award for Tesla and SolarCity
  • The Aviation Week 2008 Laureate for the most significant achievement worldwide in the space industry and National Space Society's Von Braun Trophy in 2008/2009 for leadership of the most significant achievement in space
  • Fédération Aéronautique Internationale presented Musk with in 2010 with the FAI Gold Space Medal for designing the first privately developed rocket to reach orbit
  • In 2012 he was awarded a Gold Medal from the Royal Aeronautical Society
  • In 2015 he was awarded IEEE Honorary Membership
